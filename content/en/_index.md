{{% details title="ğŸª¢ Bhai Dooj - 11 Nov" closed="true" %}}

Bhai Dooj (Also known as, Bhai Tika, Bhaubeej, Bhai Beej, Bhai Phonta or Bhratri Dwitiya)
is a festival celebrated on the second lunar day of the Shukla Paksha (bright fortnight)
of Kartika, the eighth month of the Shalivahana Shaka calendar. It is celebrated during
the Diwali or Tihar festival and Holi festival. The celebrations of this day are
similar to the festival of Raksha Bandhan.

On the day of the festival, sisters invite their brothers for a sumptuous meal often
including their favourite dishes/sweets. The whole ceremony signifies the duty of a
brother to protect his sister, as well as a sister's blessings for her brother.
